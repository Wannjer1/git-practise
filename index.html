Commit Messages

The perfect commit message is expected to be:

  1. Subject-concise summary of what happened
  2. Body-more detailed explanation
  3. what is now different than before?
  4. what's the reason for the change?
  5. Is there anything to watch out for/anything prticularly remarkable?

A basic commit structure/syntax would be
Subject-"Add captcha for email signup"(short and to the point summary of what happened)

Body- Email signups now require a capcha to be completed:
(signup.php uses our captcha library
invalid signup attempts are now blocked)
